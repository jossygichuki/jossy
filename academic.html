Back

I started schooling at an early age of three. Maybe I was a genious-I don't know. So, it was Wagura Nursery School that I first set foot on my first day in my first learning institution. Yes, it was a nursery school, not a kindergaten, i'm not a kindergaten kid. That was back in the days.

After two years, I graduated from nursery school. I joined standard one in the year 2007. Being the shortest pupil ( again not a student ), I was at the receiving end of every kind of bullying. Life was intorelable at first. However, as time crawled by, the free millet and sorghum-porridge switched my mind to adjust. So, working through thick and thin, I was through with primary education after 3-term eight good years. Luckily, I passed, just passed. Don't want to know how passed.

2016 saw me in Mukibi Educational Institute for the Sons and Daughters of African Ladies and Gentlemen.Funny as it's name, life at Mukibi was more than high school life. Yes, I never held myself. Four years down the line, I gave life my everything, no more to tell. However, inspite of eating life with a big spoon, inspite of giving life all, I passed, just passed, period.

I'm now in KU, pursuing my bacholor's degree in IT, serious than never.
